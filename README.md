    <h1>About Advanced Agriculture</h1>
    <p>
       Advanced agriculture refers to the use of modern technology, scientific research, and innovative farming methods to improve agricultural productivity. It focuses on increasing crop yield, improving quality, and reducing the negative impact on the environment. Advanced agriculture combines traditional farming knowledge with modern tools to create efficient and sustainable farming systems
    </p>

    <source src="v.mp4" type="video/mp4">

    <h2>Mission</h2>
    <p>
        The mission of advanced agriculture is to promote smart and sustainable farming practices by using modern technologies. It aims to help farmers increase productivity, reduce resource wastage, and improve crop quality while protecting natural resources such as water and soil.
    </p>

    <h2>Vision</h2>
    <p>
 The vision of advanced agriculture is to create a future where farming is efficient, environmentally friendly, and technology-driven. It seeks to ensure food security, support farmers with innovative solutions, and contribute to economic growth through sustainable agricultural development.
    </p>
